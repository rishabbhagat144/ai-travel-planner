💡 Student Saving Tips

🚂
Book trains in advance

IRCTC general or tatkal can save 40-60% vs buses on long routes.

🏕️
Choose hostels & Airbnbs

Hostels cost ₹400–800/night, often including breakfast and social vibes.

🍱
Eat where locals eat

Street food and local dhabas offer authentic meals for ₹50–150.

🎟️
Get student ID discounts

ASI monuments give 75% off to Indian students with valid ID.

📅
Travel off-season

Prices drop 30–50% in shoulder and off seasons. Check our calendar.
